How Max CPC is Calculated

Profit / Sale = AOV × Margin %
Max CPA = Profit per Sale × (1 − Overhead %)
Max CPC = Max CPA × Conversion Rate %

Example: ৳2,000 AOV × 40% margin = ৳800 profit. With 2.5% CVR → Max CPC = ৳800 × 0.025 = ৳20. If your actual CPC is below this, your campaign can be profitable.

How Min ROAS is Calculated

Min ROAS = 1 ÷ (Margin % − Overhead %) × 100%
Actual ROAS = Revenue ÷ Ad Spend × 100%
Net Profit = Revenue × Margin% − Ad Spend − Overhead Cost

Example: 40% margin with 10% overhead → effective margin = 30% → Min ROAS = 1 ÷ 0.30 × 100 = 333%. If your actual ROAS is above this, your campaign is profitable.

What are Max CPC & Min ROAS?

Every paid advertising campaign has a profitability ceiling. Max CPC tells you the absolute maximum you can pay for a click and still break even — go above it and you're burning money, go below it and you have margin room to scale.

Minimum ROAS is the flip side: the lowest return on ad spend that still covers your costs. If your campaign is below this threshold, every sale is losing you money even if you "sold something".

Max CPC Formula
AOV × Margin% × CVR% = The most you should bid per click
Min ROAS Formula
1 ÷ Effective Margin × 100 = The ROAS floor for profitability
Why it matters
Bidding without these numbers is guessing. Know your ceiling before you set any bid.
When to use
Before launching any Google Ads, Meta Ads, or eCommerce campaign.
